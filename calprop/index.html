WHAT IS THIS

The Problem

California county assessors manage millions of property parcels, each with complex ownership, exemptions, and spatial data. When natural disasters strike, they need to rapidly reassess damaged properties. Most counties still use legacy COBOL/mainframe systems from the 1980s.

CalProp is a modern replacement built from the ground up: a modular platform that handles core parcel management, automated policy enforcement, GIS-based spatial analysis, and end-to-end calamity reassessment workflows.
