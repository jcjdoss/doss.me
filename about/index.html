About This Site

The short version: I use AI to help me build and write things. Here's how.

Yes, I Use AI

This site is a collaboration. My ideas, my experiences, my perspective - but AI helped with the writing, organization, and code. I'm not hiding that because there's nothing to hide.

Here's the breakdown:

  • Ideas & Direction: All me
  • Writing & Polish: AI helps turn my rambling into sentences
  • Code & Design: AI-generated from conversations about what I wanted
  • Technical Content: My knowledge, better organized

I use spell check too. And a calculator. Tools are good.

Why I'm Telling You This

Because it's 2026 and pretending you don't use AI is like pretending you don't use Google. The interesting question isn't "do you use it" - it's "what do you use it for."

I use AI to:

  • Turn scattered thoughts into actual content
  • Build things faster than I could alone
  • Ship ideas that would otherwise stay stuck in my head
  • Handle the boring parts so I can focus on the interesting parts

Result: More stuff gets made. That's the whole point.

How This Site Was Built

I had a conversation with Claude running on OpenClaw. I said "build me a site that looks like this and does this." A couple hours later, this existed. It's pretty great.

Technical Stuff

  • Platform: OpenClaw
  • AI: Claude Sonnet 4 & Haiku 4.5
  • Frontend: Plain HTML/CSS (keeping it simple)
  • Hosting: GitHub Pages
  • Version Control: Git

Time spent: About 2 hours from "I should build a site" to "okay it's live."

On "Authenticity"

Someone's going to ask if AI-assisted content is "really me." Fair question. Here's my answer:

This is authentically me:

  • 15+ years of IT experience and opinions about it
  • The decision to build in public and be transparent about tools
  • My worldview (people are good, problems are interesting, every day's a new shot)
  • Everything I'm actually saying

This is augmented:

  • Grammar and structure
  • How fast I can turn ideas into published content
  • The code that runs this site

The ideas are mine. The polish is assisted. Same as any writer with an editor, just faster.
