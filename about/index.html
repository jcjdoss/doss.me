About This Site

Transparency about AI collaboration, content creation, and human-machine partnership.

AI Collaboration Disclosure

This site is a human-AI collaboration. Content here reflects my thoughts, experiences, and perspectives, but much of the writing, organization, and even this website's code was produced with AI assistance.

Specifically:

  • Ideas & Direction: 100% human (me)
  • Writing & Polish: AI-assisted based on my conversational input
  • Code & Design: AI-generated from my requirements and feedback
  • Technical Content: My knowledge, AI's organization and presentation

This isn't ghostwriting. It's cognitive prosthetics.

Why This Matters

ADHD brains excel at generating ideas but struggle with execution and follow-through. I have decades of experience, insights, and stories worth sharing, but the executive function required to turn thoughts into polished content often gets derailed by the next urgent task.

AI changes that equation. I can now:

  • Capture raw thoughts in conversation
  • Have AI organize and polish them
  • Review, edit, and approve the final output
  • Ship ideas that would otherwise remain stuck in my head

Result: More authentic content, not less. The ideas that matter finally make it out of my brain and into the world.

Content Creation Process

1

I talk through ideas, experiences, or technical concepts in conversation with my AI collaborator.

2

Organization

AI structures the raw thoughts into coherent narrative, maintaining my voice and perspective.

3

4

Technical Architecture

This site was built through a conversation with Claude Code running on the OpenClaw platform. The entire development process - from architectural decisions to code implementation - happened through natural language conversation.

Technology Stack

  • Platform: OpenClaw (AI agent orchestration)
  • AI Model: Claude Sonnet 4 & Haiku 4.5
  • Frontend: Vanilla HTML/CSS (brutalist design principles)
  • Hosting: GitHub Pages
  • Version Control: Git (managed by AI)

Development Time: Approximately 2 hours of conversation to go from "build me a multi-page site" to fully deployed architecture.

Authenticity in the Age of AI

Some might argue that AI assistance makes content "less authentic." I disagree.

What's authentic:

  • The lived experiences I'm sharing
  • The technical insights from 15+ years in IT
  • The ADHD struggles and solutions
  • The decision to build in public despite executive dysfunction

What's augmented:

  • The grammar and structure of my thoughts
  • The organization of complex ideas
  • The consistency of voice across different content types
  • The speed from concept to published content

Using AI to overcome executive function barriers doesn't make the underlying thoughts less valid. It makes them more accessible.
