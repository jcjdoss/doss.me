üéØ The PDeLR Framework I'm Testing

Instead of just having ideas and hoping I follow through, I'm applying this systematic approach:

P - Priority:
Who's waiting? What breaks if I don't do this? Force clarity on what actually matters vs what feels urgent.
D - Details:
What exactly needs to happen? ADHD brains skip details then get stuck. Force myself to be specific upfront.
L - Logic:
Why this approach? What's the reasoning chain? Prevents getting lost in execution when motivation fades.
R - Recurring:
What's the daily/weekly rhythm? How does this become a system instead of a one-off burst?
