üéØ PDeLR: A Framework for ADHD Life Management

Instead of fighting my brain's wiring, I built a systematic approach that works WITH how ADHD cognition actually functions:

P - Priority:
WHO'S WAITING? WHAT BREAKS? Force clarity on what actually matters vs what feels urgent. ADHD brains are terrible at priority assessment - make it systematic.
D - Details:
What exactly needs to happen? ADHD brains skip details then get stuck. External scaffolding forces specificity before execution begins.
L - Logic:
Why this approach? What's the reasoning chain? Prevents getting lost in execution when ADHD motivation inevitably fades.
R - Recurring:
How does this become sustainable? ADHD requires external systems for long-term maintenance. Build the maintenance INTO the solution.
